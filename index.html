WHAT IS A DEADLIFT?

The deadlift is an excellent compound
exercise that targets the quads, hamstrings,
gluteal muscles, lower back, traps,
and forearms—not to mention it will
make you feel like a beast
when you do it.

PROPER DEADLIFT

To Deadlift with proper form means with
your lower back neutral. Proper
Deadlift form increases effectiveness.
You’ll get stronger and build more
muscle if you Deadlift with proper form.

IMPROPER DEADLIFT

Well for starters, everybody thinks of
Deadlifts as a back exercise—which it
is—but unlike many compound or
isolated workouts, there are
a lot of additional parts that
couple this back building powerhouse.
